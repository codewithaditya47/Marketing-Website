About Us

Founded in 2016, Sai Polymers has grown into a reliable manufacturer of laminated rolls, multilayer films, and printed pouches for food and FMCG industries.


With almost 10 years of experience, we understand how important packaging is in:

  • protecting product quality
  • maintaining hygiene
  • retaining aroma and freshness
  • building brand value

High-quality laminated rolls, multilayer films,and printed pouches designed to protect freshness, ensure hygiene, and extend shelf life.


Our Company Story

At first, we found that the packaging industry has great potential in the future market. So, we decided to enter this industry with the goal of providing dependable, high-quality packaging solutions to businesses across India. Since then, Sai Polymers has continuously improved its capability, technology, and product range.

Sai Polymers is a trusted flexible packaging manufacturer delivering food-grade polymer packaging to B2B manufacturers and distributors across India. Our products provide excellent strength, leak resistance, and premium printing quality so your brand stands out on shelves.
